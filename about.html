About Global Skills Hub

We’re an international, yet close-knit team of HR professionals based in Toronto.

Each day we come to work to solve one of the biggest challenges for highgrowth companies across Canada: the shortage of highly-skilled and experienced technical talent.

We help Canadian companies find, select, hire, and then settle the very best talent from around the world. We’re inspired by the talented and courageous individuals from around the world who are looking for better opportunities for themselves and their families, and we believe that Canada is the best place for them to live and grow.
